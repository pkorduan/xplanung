A ScopedName is a composite of a LocalName for anther NameSpace and a GenericName valid in that NameSpace.<br/><br/>This is the value returned by the getObject() operations. In a cascade for of TypeNames and Attribute names, the usual notation is a sequence of name separated by dots ".".<br/><p/>
